About Wave Cafe

Alexandria Cafe Shop and Gallery offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in Alexandria . We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee (although you are more than welcome to do that), we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you. If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area In front of the sea that is perfect for your weary mind and body.

We offer a delicious variety of coffee from Alexandria Cafe made by our professionally trained baristas. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages. All of our sauces & syrups are made in-house with all natural ingredients (no chemicals or preservatives) ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health. You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker. We look forward to serving you at Alexandria Cafe.
